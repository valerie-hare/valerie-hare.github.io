About Me

Copywriter & Web Developer

Ever since tenth grade AP English class, I have wanted to be a writer. My English teacher was one of the first to recognize my passion, talent, and dedication to copywriting. Fast forward to my first degree, I majored in writing, where I received glowing feedback from my peers and professors about my wordsmithing abilities. Throughout my life, I have developed this art of writing to communicate my thoughts, feelings, and wisdom to the world. From writing personal blogs in college to crafting SEO content for everything from start-ups and small businesses to B2C and B2B businesses, I’ve learned that effective written communication is at the core of any successful organization.

My passion for web development began after the birth of Myspace. My best friend and I spent countless hours building Myspace layouts for fun. In 2016, I took a web design class, fueling my coding interest even further. This led to my second degree - in web development. From coding social media layouts and building start-up websites for local businesses to redesigning outdated B2B SaaS websites and managing WordPress sites for clients, I’ve learned that optimized, accessible, and on-brand websites are essential for every business.

Because of my creative and technical skill sets and experience, I’ve been called a unicorn and superhero. My mission is to leverage my seven-plus years of copywriting and web development expertise to make the world a better place one piece of code and word at a time. I’m passionate about helping businesses develop unique digital brands via stellar copy and websites.

Outside of work, I’m a beach dweller who loves animals, nature, family, good food, and healthy living.
