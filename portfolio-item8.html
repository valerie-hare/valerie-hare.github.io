Personal site

View Site

In fall 2016, I designed a personal website for my Web Design class at Drury University. Wix.com was used to create this site about my personal journey with GAD (Generalized Anxiety Disorder). For the site, I used a cool blue color scheme and calming graphics of nature to create a relaxing vibe. Like any site, I followed the basic web design rules of balance, proximity, alignment, focus, color, and contrast. This helped established a visual pleasing layout, organized content, functional site navigation, and easy-to-read content. This site includes relevant content for managing anxiety naturally. For example, I wrote a post that shares 10 tips for reducing anxiety that I've learned from experience and online resources.
