Kayaking for Epilepsywebsite

Charity site

View Site

In April 2017, I built and designed a charity site for the VFW Post 6280's Kayaking for Epilepsy charity event. This was a 3 month long project, where I worked directly with this group's commander on their project needs and requirements. For the site content, I created a registration form and Paypal widget, so site visitors could register for this event online. I added a calendar and map widget for registration deadlines and the event's location.

Additionally, I wrote various content such as blog posts related to epilepsy facts and first aid. I added educational videos to enhance the site's mission to promote epilepsy awareness. I uploaded testimonials from people affected by epilepsy to help inspire others and raise awareness
